Our History



How did it all start?

Once upon a time, in a big city called Leeds, there was Mr.Hooch who wanted to rent a bike. As a lazy student who had to travel all the way from Headingly to Campus, he wanted to make his life easier by renting a bike. However he couldn’t find a website which summed up all rentals available in the city. “How is this possible?” Mr.Hooch thought, “I have to make this a reality, that anyone can rent a bike anywhere, anytime!”. You guessed it right, that’s how UniCylce started! Mr. Hooch opended up a small bike rental business, and now he is Mr.Worldwide!


Who are we?

We are a team of students from the University of Leeds, under Mr.Hooch's management. This young, enthusiastic, and diverse team consists of English, Dutch, South African, Indian and Korean inviduals. We thrive to facilitate the people of Leeds in the comfort of travelling :)
